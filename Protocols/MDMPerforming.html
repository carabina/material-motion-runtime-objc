MDMPerforming

@protocol MDMPerforming <NSObject>

A class conforming to MDMPerforming is expected to implement the plan of motion described by objects that conform to MDMPlan.

Designated initializer
  • -initWithTarget:

    The receiver is expected to execute its plan to the provided target.
